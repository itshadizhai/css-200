## Problem Being Solved
Students receive important academic and financial information across multiple platforms (Outlook/email, LMS like D2L, and campus portals). Because messages are scattered and mixed with non-urgent content, critical items get buried.

This causes:
- missed deadlines and lost opportunities  
- extra time spent searching for information  
- stress and information overload  

---

## System Vision
**Vision:** A unified, AI-powered inbox that organizes and prioritizes academic communications so students never miss what matters.

**What the system will do:**
- Connect email, LMS, and campus platforms into one student view
- Automatically categorize messages into:
  - Finance
  - Assignments & Deadlines
  - Jobs & Opportunities
  - Events
  - Other
- Detect priority (High / Medium / Low) using sender credibility, message content, and embedded deadlines
- Surface what matters in one place:
  - Today’s Priorities
  - Financial Alerts (tuition bills, refunds, scholarship deadlines)
  - Opportunities (internships/scholarships/jobs matched to profile + deadlines)
- Take automated actions:
  - add deadlines to calendar with smart reminders
  - send push/email nudges before cutoffs
  - generate weekly summaries and missed-item alerts

**Success looks like:**
- fewer missed deadlines and fewer lost opportunities  
- reduced student stress and cognitive load  
- faster decisions and higher trust in campus communication  

---

## System Overview (High-Level)
The AI Academic Command Center creates one unified experience for students:

1. **Ingest messages** from Outlook/email, LMS (D2L), and campus platforms  
2. **Classify categories** (Finance, Assignments, Opportunities, Events, Other)  
3. **Detect priority** (High/Medium/Low) based on deadlines + content + credibility  
4. **Surface priorities** in a single dashboard view:
   - Today’s Priorities
   - Financial Alerts
   - Opportunities
